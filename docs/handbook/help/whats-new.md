**What's New** is the in-app record of what changed in each release of
the system - new features, improvements, and fixes - written in plain
language for the people who use the app, not a developer changelog.

:::video help-and-whats-new

## Finding it

Open the user menu (your name, top-right) and choose **What's New**. A
small dot appears next to that menu item whenever there is a release you
have not read yet; the dot clears once you open the page.

## Reading the feed

`/whats-new` lists every release newest-first. Each entry shows:

- the **version** and **date** of the release,
- a one-line **summary** of the headline change, and
- the full notes for that release - grouped into what's new, what
  improved, and what was fixed.

The most recent release is expanded at the top so you see the latest
changes first; older releases follow in order.

## Where the notes come from

Release notes are maintained by the product team and ship with each
update - there is nothing to configure. When the system is upgraded, the
new release's notes appear here automatically and the unread dot returns
for everyone until they look.

This page answers the *release management* expectation: every customer
can see, inside the app, exactly what changed and when.

The **equipment catalog** is the list of items and services exhibitors can order
through their portal. Manage it under **Admin -> Equipment catalog**.

:::video equipment-catalog-editor

## Adding & editing items

Add an item with its **category**, **SKU**, **name**, **unit label** (each, day,
...), and **standard price**. You can also set an optional **advance price** - the
discounted early-order rate (applied before an event's advance-rate deadline; see
[Exhibitor orders](/docs/exhibitors/orders)). Click **Edit** on any row to change
it, and toggle **Active/Inactive** to show or hide an item from the portal
without deleting its history.

Managing the catalog needs administrator access.

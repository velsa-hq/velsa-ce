This release adds self-service help, the page you're reading now, and a
new compliance module.

### New

- **What's New.** This page - an in-app, plain-language record of what
  changed in each release, with an unread indicator in the user menu.
- **Get help.** Send a question, problem, or suggestion from inside the
  app (user menu -> Get help). Requests are recorded for staff to triage
  under Admin -> Support requests, and can optionally email your support
  contacts.
- **Insurance certificate tracking.** Record and review Certificates of
  Insurance (COI) for clients and exhibitors - coverage details, the
  document, a review decision, and an expiry date the system watches.
  Exhibitors can upload their own certificate from the portal, and a
  nightly job flags anything expiring soon. Find it under Admin ->
  Insurance certificates.

### Improved

- Ongoing security hardening: optional concurrent-session limits,
  password-lifetime policy, and account-change notifications (all
  off by default, for environments that require them).
